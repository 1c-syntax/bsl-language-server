# Diagnostics

Highlights errors, potential issues and coding-standard violations inline and in the Problems panel.

**Shortcut:** `Ctrl+Shift+M (Problems panel)`

[← All features](index.md)

The user opens the `demo.bsl` module and presses Ctrl+Shift+M to open the Problems panel. The problematic spots are highlighted inline in the code, and the Problems panel lists the detected diagnostics with their descriptions.
