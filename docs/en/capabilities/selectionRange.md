# Smart selection

Expand and shrink the selection step by step along syntactic boundaries.

**Shortcut:** `Shift+Alt+→ / ←`

[← All features](index.md)

The cursor sits inside a sub-expression of an `Если` condition in `selection.bsl`, and the user presses `Shift+Alt+→` several times. The selection grows step by step along syntactic boundaries — from the sub-expression to the condition, then the `Если` block, the procedure body and the whole file.
