# Rename

Safely rename a symbol together with all its usages.

**Shortcut:** `F2`

[← All features](index.md)

The cursor is placed on a method, `F2` is pressed, the new name `Суммировать` is typed and confirmed with `Enter`. The method and all of its usages are renamed at once.
